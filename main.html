Welcome To Citrus

Welcome to a chain of distinctive hotels and resorts that are bench-marked against the best in the world. Discover #HappinessMoments at Citrus Resorts and Hotels in Sri Lanka and take with you memories that are bound to bring you back.

CITRUS HOTELS AND LOCATIONS

CITRUS HOTELS ABOUT HOTELS IMAGES LOCATION
CITRUS Waskaduwa Citrus Waskaduwa, a five-star resort on the west coast of Sri Lanka, offers luxury beach vacations with stunning views, outstanding cuisine, and accommodation. Located 37km from Colombo, it boasts an unspoiled 400m long beachfront.

VISIT
CITRUS Hikkaduwa Explore the eclectic paradise of Citrus Hikkaduwa, located in southern Sri Lanka, offering ideal accommodations for beach vacations. Known for its shallow fringing reef, Hikkaduwa is perfect for water sports like snorkeling, scuba diving, and surfing.

VISIT
The Steuart by CITRUS The Steuart House, once the headquarters of George Steuart & Co. Ltd, was transformed into a boutique business hotel in Colombo 01 in 2015. The Steuart by Citrus offers 50 rooms on eight floors, showcasing Scottish style and providing a unique urban escape.

VISIT
Villa CITRUS Victoria Golf Club Villa Citrus, Digana, a contemporary-style homestay, offers luxury accommodation, delicious food, and a variety of holiday activities, including golf, nature walks, and cultural tours. The property features a swimming pool, pool table, library, TV room, and outdoor spaces.

VISIT
Villa CITRUS Labugolla Villa Citrus, a colonial-style bungalow, overlooks Dedunupitiya and Sabaragamuwa province. Built by British citizen Mr. Winter, it features 6 luxurious rooms in Labugolla Private Sanctuary, surrounded by tropical flora and fauna.
